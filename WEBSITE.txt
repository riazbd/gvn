About Us
GVN Consortium (Global Visa Network) is a trusted EU work visa consultancy based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Since 2019,
we have been helping skilled and semi-skilled professionals secure legitimate employment opportunities in Serbia and
 Slovenia—two of Europe’s most rapidly growing labor markets.
With over 350+ successful EU work visas processed, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for
individuals looking to build a better career abroad. Our team follows a transparent, ethical, and
documentation-accurate approach to ensure that every client receives professional guidance and a smooth visa
experience.
From initial assessment to employer selection, documentation support, and final visa submission, we provide a fully
structured pathway for applicants who aspire to work and grow in Europe with confidence.
At GVN Consortium, your journey begins with trust—powered by real opportunities, authentic processing, and a
commitment to your future.

Mission
To provide transparent, ethical, and reliable EU work visa solutions that help Bangladeshi professionals access
genuine employment opportunities in Serbia and Slovenia, supported by fast processing, accurate documentation, and
premium client service.

Vision
To become Bangladesh’s most trusted and professional EU visa consultancy by delivering high-quality services,
ensuring client confidence, and creating a seamless bridge between skilled workers and European employers.

Our Services
EU Work Permit Visa (Serbia & Slovenia)
We provide complete support for authentic EU work permit visas in Serbia and Slovenia. From eligibility checks to
employer matching, documentation, and embassy submission — we guide you through every stage with transparency and
accuracy.

Skilled Migration Support
Our skilled migration service helps professionals move abroad through genuine opportunities that match their
qualifications and experience. We assist with profile evaluation, document preparation, job alignment, and migration
 pathway guidance.

Visit Visa Assistance
Whether for tourism, business trips, or short-term travel, we help you prepare and process your visit visa
applications with properly aligned documents and professional guidance to increase your approval chances.

Employer Matching & Job Placement
We connect applicants with verified employers in Europe, ensuring genuine job opportunities across multiple sectors
such as manufacturing, logistics, hospitality, agriculture, and construction.

Document Preparation & Verification
We prepare, verify, and organize all necessary documentation — including passports, CVs, employment letters, and
visa forms — ensuring compliance with embassy and employer requirements.

Visa Application Submission
Our experienced consultants manage the full visa submission process, embassy scheduling, and file preparation to
prevent delays, errors, or rejections.

Pre-Departure & Post-Visa Guidance
We provide guidance on travel, accommodation, workplace expectations, cultural orientation, and essential do’s &
don’ts to ensure a smooth transition to your new life abroad.
